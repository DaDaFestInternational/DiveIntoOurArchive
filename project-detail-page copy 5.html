Touretteshero / Jess Thom, Not I at the Bluecoat in 2018

Baluji Shrivastav at The Picket, Jordan Street, 2007

Jess Thom performed as Mouth in this ground-breaking theatre piece, taking on Samuel Beckett’s challenging monologue and remaking it as potent disability art. This production demonstrated how integrated access for artists and audiences enhances and brings deeper meaning to well-known work.

Baluji Shrivastav is an Indian / British musician and instrumentalist, who works internationally. He has become one of the world’s leading composers and sitar players and has worked with a wide variety of artists including Stevie Wonder, Coldplay, Massive Attack, Madness, Shakira, Malu, Boy George & Doves.

“Curating the DaDa exhibition was an inspiring experience, seeing so many talented disabled individuals captured in the midst of their performances.” - Scout Dragon
