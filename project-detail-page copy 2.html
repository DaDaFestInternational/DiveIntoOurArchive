Mat Fraser

A dynamic, charismatic performer, Mat Fraser has appeared at DaDaFest in his own productions as well as in the role of a quick-witted and entertaining cabaret compere. Here he is at DaDaFest in 2008.

Mat has gone on to appear in many popular television and streaming series, working in the UK and the US. Like many of our alumni, he is proud to return to his roots in disability arts and activism.
